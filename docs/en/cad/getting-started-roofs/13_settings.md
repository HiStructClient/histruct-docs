# ⚙️Settings - Customize Your Modeling Space

HiStruct is built for flexibility, letting you tailor the modeling experience to your needs. Using the **Settings** **button** in left-side menu, you can customize modeling functions and adjust parameters in the modeling space.

**Settings step by step**

**Input: You can choose between two modes:**

- **Basic**: A simplified set of tools and functions for faster modeling - perfect for straightforward projects or when you want to work quickly.

- **Advanced**: Unlocks all available functions, giving full control over every aspect of the modeling space. Ideal for complex projects or detailed customizations.

**Scene Settings**

- **Terrain Surface**: Select the appearance of the surrounding terrain in the modeling space.

- **Automatic Dimensions**: Enable plan dimensions of the object to be rendered automatically. You can later control their display manually using the buttons on the right side of the screen.

- **Scale of Add Buttons**: Adjust the size of buttons which are placed on the model.

- **Insert LCS Symbol Size**: Adjust the size of the coordinate system marker in the model.

**Project Data**

Project data is loaded from the project location, if specified, ensuring all relevant information is automatically applied to your model.

**💡 Switching between Basic and Advanced mode lets you balance speed and functionality depending on your project's complexity. Combine this with automatic updates for drawings, BOMs, and reports, and you have a workflow that's fast, precise, and fully adaptable.**
